Hares and Hyenas

Hares and Hyenas bookshop specialises in queer identity, sexuality, sex and gender diversity. Intersecting social factors such as gender, class, race and cultural background are used to develop a broad collection of titles based on contemporary lived experience outside the heteronormative mainstream. Put simply? We're queer.
